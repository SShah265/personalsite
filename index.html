Overland Camping Rooftop Shower

I built this rooftop water tank for camping trips with the help of my friends. This was the first time it was mounted on my truck. We ended up added foam kayak blocks and racheting tie downs instead of bungee cords to ensure that it did not move. I designed it to provide about 8 gallons of water and with the shower head, up to a 5 minute shower. It's extremely stable and has not reduced the fuel economy of the truck by a drastic amount.

Watch Winder

A watch winder I designed and 3D printed from the ground up. A small stepper motor attaches to the shaft behind the plate which turns the gears, which in turn spins the watch so that it remains wound even when I am not wearing it.

I designed these little tokens in Adobe Illustrator and lasercut them to be handed out during an FTC robotics tournament hosted at my high school.
